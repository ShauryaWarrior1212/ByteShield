Essential Cybersecurity Tips

1. Regularly Update Your Software

Keeping your software up-to-date helps protect your system from vulnerabilities and exploits that hackers may use.

2. Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Always use strong passwords that combine letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password across multiple sites.

3. Be Wary of Phishing Scams

Don't click on links or download attachments from unknown or suspicious emails to avoid falling victim to phishing scams.
